Our History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into a globally recognized event, drawing thousands of attendees and hosting groundbreaking presentations across software, AI, robotics, and emerging technologies.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to ignite innovation, foster collaboration, and provide a platform for bold thinkers shaping the future. We aim to bridge the gap between visionary ideas and real-world implementation through impactful sessions and hands-on experiences.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Lisa Wong

A leader in AI ethics and machine learning, Dr. Wong has shaped global policy on responsible AI. Her keynote in 2022 emphasized ethical design in emerging systems.

Jamal Carter

Jamal is a tech entrepreneur and cloud computing expert. In 2023, he inspired audiences with his journey from startup founder to global innovator in distributed systems.

Elena Ruiz

Elena is a UX pioneer advocating for inclusive and accessible tech design. Her workshop in 2021 remains one of the most-watched sessions in TechCon history.
